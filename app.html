Functionality #1 - Create your user profile


With the ZOPA application, you can create a user profile allowing you to save all the data you want. Through your profile, you can manage and save your usage preferences. Find out what foods you're allergic to, or even just don't like.







Functionality #2 – Scan your products


It has never been easier to scan a product. Thanks to your smartphone and ZOPA, you can scan all your products and be notified in real time. You just need an internet connection, target the product barcode, and you're done!







Functionality #3 – Avoid any danger, any allergens


This is the very heart of our app! As soon as you scan a product, you will be notified in real time by a smiley. Green is fine, red is danger.







Functionality #4 – Save your favorites


You want to save certain products, with or without allergens ... It is possible ! Thanks to our favorites manager, nothing could be simpler! This will help you remember what to stop buying or even surprise your friends about the content of a product !







Functionality #5 – Add missing products


ZOPA operates through a self-service database on the net. It is possible that certain products are not informed. Do not panic ! All you have to do is add the product in question and fill in its characteristics. Our team will then take care of validating the registration (or not).







Functionality #6 – Exchange products with users close to you


In addition, ZOPA is aware that a product that is dangerous for you will not end up on your plate. You then have at your disposal in the application, the means of contacting other users to negotiate exchanges of products. The ZOPA app is also a commitment to economics and ecology.
